Post-doctoral Researcher Miikka Kuutila, PhD, Dalhousie University

I am postdoctoral research fellow at Dalhousie University. I am part of Paul Ralph's lab Dalsel.

Previously I worked as a research assistant, doctoral student and post-doctoral researcher at Empirical Software Engineering in Software, Systems and Services (M3S) research unit at the University of Oulu.
